# Accessing the Platform:

You can access Proxus by navigating to `http://localhost:8080` from your web browser.

The default username is `Admin`, and the password field should be left blank.

# Overview of the Proxus IIoT Platform:

The Proxus IIoT Platform provides a variety of features designed to help you manage your IoT devices and data. Below, you'll find a brief description of each main feature:

## Data Management:

- **Devices:** Manage your IoT devices from a centralized location.

- **Device Profiles:** Create and manage profiles for different types of devices.

- **Edge Gateways:** Manage the gateways that serve as an intermediary for data flow between your devices and the cloud.

- **Device Raw Data:** View and manage the raw data received from your devices.

## Rule Management:

- **Rules:** Set up rules to automate actions based on specific conditions in your device data.

- **Rule Actions:** Define the actions that will be taken when your rules are triggered.

- **Notification Channels:** Set up channels through which you will receive notifications when rules are triggered.

## Integrations:

- **Target Channels:** Define the channels to which your data will be sent for further processing or storage.

## Functions:

- **User Functions:** Write custom functions to transform your data, implement business logic, or integrate with other systems.

## Visualization:

- **Dashboards:** Create dashboards to visualize your device data and gain insights.

- **Reports:** Generate reports based on your device data.

## System:

- **Users:** Manage users who have access to the Proxus IIoT Platform.

- **Log:** View logs of system activities.

- **My Details:** View and update your user profile details.

- **Role:** Manage roles and permissions for users in the Proxus IIoT Platform.

## License:

- **License Info:** View information about your Proxus IIoT Platform license.
